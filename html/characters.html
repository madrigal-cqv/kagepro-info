Characters

Ayano Tateyama

No. 0

Ayano Tateyama (楯山 文乃 Tateyama Ayano) / Ayano (アヤノ) was a classmate and friend of Shintaro Kisaragi, the underclassman of Takane Enomoto and Haruka Kokonose and the daughter of Kenjirou Tateyama and Ayaka Tateyama. She is the original founder of the Mekakushi Dan as Member No. 0 and the older foster sister of Tsubomi Kido, Kousuke Seto and Shuuya Kano. Her eye ability, Favoring Eyes (目をかける Me wo Kakeru), allows her to project her and possibly others' thoughts and memories to other people, which she refers to as "the power of caring".

Tsubomi Kido

No. 1

Tsubomi Kido (木戸 つぼみ Kido Tsubomi) / Kido (キド) is the first member of the Mekakushi Dan and currently its leader. She is the biological younger half-sister of Rin Kido. She is the foster sister of Seto, Kano, and Ayano Tateyama. Her eye ability, Concealing Eyes (目を隠す Me o Kakusu), allows her to divert perception away from certain people and/or objects within 2 meters of themselves, making them difficult to identify and practically invisible. Conversation between hidden people won't be heard by outsiders; however, physical contact breaks the effect. Her ability also allows her to conceal her own memories from herself.

Kousuke Seto

No. 2

Kousuke Seto (瀬戸 幸助 Seto Kōsuke) / Seto (セト) is the second member of the Mekakushi Dan. He is the foster brother of Kido, Kano, and Ayano Tateyama. and also Mary Kozakura's first friend. His eye ability, Stealing Eyes (目を盗む Me o Nusumu), allows him to hear the thoughts of others, including animals. When used to its full potential, it can read a person's memories and experiences.

Shuuya Kano

No. 3

Shuuya Kano (鹿野 修哉 Kano Shūya) / Kano (カノ) is the third member of the Mekakushi Dan. He's also the one who came up with the name Mekakushi Dan on his own initiative. He is the foster brother of Kido, Seto, and Ayano Tateyama. His eye ability, Deceiving Eyes (目を欺く Me wo Azamuku), allows him to change what people perceive him as; he can change how he appears, sounds, and even feels to others and can thus impersonate other people and animals. He can only use this ability on himself and not others.

Mary Kozakura

No. 4

Mary Kozakura (小桜 茉莉 Kozakura Marī) / Mary (マリー) is the fourth member of the Mekakushi Dan. She is the daughter of Shion, as well as the granddaughter of Azami and Tsukihiko. Mary has a power called "Locking Eyes", which she inherited from her mother Shion and her grandmother Azami. It allows her to temporarily paralyze whoever meets her gaze. It is not as complete as her mother's or grandmother's power, as she is not able to turn people into stone, but it can instead make them appear frozen for a few minutes.

Momo Kisaragi

No. 5

Momo Kisaragi (如月 桃 Kisaragi Momo) / Momo (モモ) is the fifth member of the Mekakushi Dan, the little sister of Shintaro Kisaragi and a recently new idol who is a first-year in high-schooler in 10th grade. Her eye ability, Drawing Eyes (目を奪う Me o Ubau), allows her to draw peoples' attention to herself regardless of their preferences in tastes or interests. She is also able to tell where a person's attention is drawn to.

Ene

No. 6

Ene (エネ) is a cyber girl who lives in Shintaro's computer and becomes the sixth member of the Mekakushi Dan. She is secretly his former schoolmate Takane Enomoto (榎本 貴音 Enomoto Takane) who lost her body two years ago. She was the classmate of Haruka, student of Kenjirou, and upperclassman of both Shintaro and Ayano. Her eye ability, Opening Eyes (目が覚める Me ga Sameru), allows her to split her spirit from her body, which then can reside in electronic devices (such as a cellphone) as a cyber-being. Although she can be deleted from a device, she will reappear as long as it is connected to the Internet.

Shintaro Kisaragi

No. 7

Shintaro Kisaragi (如月 伸太郎 Kisaragi Shintarō) / Shintaro (シンタロー) is the main protagonist of the series. A digital girl named Ene that lives inside his computer causes him to go outside for the first time in two years, where he meets the Mekakushi Dan and becomes its seventh member.

Amamiya Hibiya

No. 8

Hibiya Amamiya (雨宮 響也 Amamiya Hibiya) / Hibiya (ヒビヤ) is the eighth member of the Mekakushi Dan. During the present-day plot of the series, he and his schoolmate Hiyori Asahina are visiting the city for a summer trip and are staying at her brother-in-law's residence, where they meet Konoha. His eye ability, Focusing Eyes (目を凝らす Me wo Korasu), has the power to perceive objects and details that are far away from an aerial view by focus.

Konoha

No. 9

Konoha (コノハ) is the the 9th member of the Mekakushi Dan. He is a mysterious man with apparent amnesia who is staying at Kenjirou's residence, where he meets Hibiya and Hiyori. His eye ability, Awakening Eyes (目を醒ます Me wo Samasu), gives him the power to remake his body into one that its true owner, Haruka Kokonose, finds to be "ideal". Since Haruka was physically weak and sickly, this caused Konoha to become supernaturally strong.
