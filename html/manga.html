Kagerou Daze (カゲロウデイズ Kagerō Deizu) is a manga series written by Jin (Shizen no Teki-P) and illustrated by Mahiro Satou as well as the sequel to the Music Route. It was serialized in the Japanese magazine Monthly Comic Gene from June 15, 2012 to February 15, 2019. After the first 4 chapters were released, Media Factory published the first official volume of the manga. The series, along with the light novels, is licensed by Yen Press in North America. The manga entered its climax during the 11th volume and ended with the 13th volume. The story is followed by the Novel Route.
